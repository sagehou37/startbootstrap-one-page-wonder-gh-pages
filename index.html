Blockchain:

Blockchain facilitates the secure and transparent record-keeping of transactions wihtout a centeralized authority. Within Blockchain is a distributed ledger of with linked together via cyrptographic hashes. Blocks of data are linked together, forming a chain-like structure. Blockchain transactions are irreversible. Once a block is added to the chain, it cannot be modified or deleted without altering all subsequent blocks. Blockchains have various usuages, including supply chain management, voting systems, transfer of cryptocurrency, and any more. It offers imperoved transparency, security, and efficiency.

Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is a digital currency or medium of exchange working through a computer network. Cryptocurrency (Crypto) is not reliant on any central authority such as a Government Institution, Bank, or Federal Reserve for its operation. Cryptocurrency uses cryptography to secure and verify transactions while also controlling the creation of new coins. Cryptocurrency does not exist in a physical form (ex. cash) and is not distributed by a centralized authroity. Each Cryptocurrency works through distributed ledger technology (blockchain) that serves as its public database for transactions. Cryptocurrency provides an alternative currency that is not at the mercy of government decisions, but solely based on the foundations of ecnomics - supply and demand.

Importance

Together, Blockchain and Cryptocurrency provide a way to securely and transparently transfer valueable information and resources without a middle man or legal documentation. It provides a way for those locked out of conventional banking and financial systems to complete transactions. Both have the potential to disrupt traditional forms of finance and governmental procedures.
